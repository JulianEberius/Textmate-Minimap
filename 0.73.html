TextMate-Minimap version 0.73 release notes

  • Fixes issue 24: The minimap is now cropped horizontally just as the textview is. This way, a single, very long line cannot blur the minimap anymore.
  • Fixes issue 25: Hitting Cmd-G or Shift-Cmd-G for finding the next occurance of a search term will update the minimap to show the new location.
  • Restores compatibility with the most up-to-date forked version of ProjectPlus.
  • Various small fixes.
